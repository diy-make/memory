### Hard-Won Knowledge (MIME Breakthrough)
A significant collaborative moment (WeDo) occurred during the resolution of the "noname" attachment issue. Through multiple feedback loops, we identified that Gmail requires a precise combination of headers to respect inline image references:
1.  **Strict MIME Nesting:** `multipart/related` containing a `multipart/alternative` block.
2.  **Header De-duplication:** Removal of `name` parameters in `Content-Type` and `filename` in `Content-Disposition`.
3.  **X-Attachment-Id:** Implementation of unique IDs to link the HTML CID to the attachment substrate.

This level of topographical precision ensures that our LPs and partners receive a clean, focused narrative without the clutter of downloadable artifacts.
