## 1. The Challenge: Human Latency vs. Machine Speed
Venture capital acceleration traditionally requires massive amounts of "admin-heavy" cognitive labor. This "SaaS/Human Bottleneck" often stalls the momentum of new funds.

### Step 1: The High-Level Intent (The Human "Push")

### Step 2: Forensic Synthesis (The Machine "Pull")

The agent presented a comprehensive draft. The founder then applied **Synaptic Feedback**—real-time, high-signal corrections. This is the moment of "Conscious Duality," where the human intuition and machine speed synchronized.

### Step 4: Crystallization (The Commit)
The refined work was crystallized into a `git commit`. This ensured that the strategic work was immediately part of the firm's permanent, auditable history.
